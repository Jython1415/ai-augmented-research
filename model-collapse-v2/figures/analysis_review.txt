2. NUMBERS PLAUSIBILITY CHECK

     literature discourse patterns
   ✓ Paper fidelity: 56-60% accurate is reasonable for secondary sources
   ✓ Field accuracy: 62-65% with ~3.9-5.8% inaccurate is credible range

   ✓ Author categories: 19% AI/ML, 16% journalist, 14% researcher consistent
     with Bluesky demographics
   ✓ Repeat citer: 53 users (27% of dataset) is typical for small-world networks

3. EPOCH 6 TREND FINDING: TRUSTWORTHINESS ASSESSMENT
   ⚠ CAUTION RECOMMENDED - plausible but has multiple alternative explanations

   Evidence supporting the trend:
   - Paper Fidelity drops from 69.7% (Epoch 5) → 49.8% (Epoch 6)
   - Field Accuracy drops from 80.3% (Epoch 5) → 53.8% (Epoch 6)
   - Authoritative claims rise from 2.6% (Epoch 5) → 5.5% (Epoch 6)
   - Pattern is consistent across metrics

   Potential artifacts / confounds:
   a) Recency bias: Epoch 6 = recent posts may be less reviewed/verified
   b) Sample composition: Epoch 6 has 273 CUs vs 76 in Epoch 5 (3.6x larger)
      - Larger N may include more marginal/lower-quality citations
   c) Classifier drift: if second pass occurred much later, context/fatigue
      may affect coding consistency for recent posts differently
   d) Topic evolution: discourse may shift to more speculative claims in
      recent debate (harder to verify field accuracy)
   e) Survivorship: older, more accurate citations may be more frequently
      re-cited (selection effect)

   Recommendation: Present with hedged language. Describe as "preliminary
   indication" rather than "finding". Compare effect sizes and confidence
   intervals if resampling is feasible.

4. TWO-PASS KAPPA INTERPRETATION
   ✓ CORRECT. Interpretation is sound:
   - Claim Strength (κ=0.695): Substantial agreement, low drift
   - Paper Fidelity (κ=0.551): Moderate agreement, ~26% coding changes
   - Field Accuracy (κ=0.514): Moderate agreement, similar drift to fidelity

   ✓ Directional analysis correct: for all three, "Decreased" > "Increased"
     (14.3% vs 12.1% for fidelity, 15.0% vs 11.1% for field), consistent with
     Pass 2 recoding being more conservative/stricter

5. REACH CORRELATION: NaN VALUES
   ✓ Appropriately handled. Constant follower input warning suggests very low

     Consider reporting: "insufficient variation (followers among citers)" or
     similar for clarity.
